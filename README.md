# Who to Chase Today

Know who to follow up, what to prioritise, and where your risk is — using a simple Excel + Power BI workflow.

---

## The problem

Most small businesses don’t struggle with unpaid invoices because they lack data.

They struggle because:
- it’s not clear who to chase first  
- everything looks equally important  
- risk isn’t obvious  

---

## What this does

This turns your unpaid invoices into a **clear daily action plan**.

It helps you:
- see who to chase today  
- focus on the invoices that matter  
- identify customers that might not pay  
- understand where your risk sits  

---

## How it works

1. Export your invoices from your system (Xero, MYOB, QuickBooks, etc.)  
2. Paste into the Excel template  
3. Open the dashboard and click **Refresh**

That’s it.
